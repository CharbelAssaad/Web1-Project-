About Us:

Savior Highschool is a leading institution for education in the local community. Founded in 1968, we have a long history of providing students with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in today's world. Our dedicated faculty and staff are committed to creating a supportive and inclusive learning environment for all students. We offer a wide range of academic programs, including:
  • Mathematics
  • Science (biology, chemistry, physics)
  • English language arts
  • Social studies (history, geography, civics)
  • Foreign languages (Spanish, French, German, Chinese, etc.)
  • Fine arts (art, music, theater)
  • Physical education
  • Computer science
  • Business studies
  • Economics
  • Psychology
  • Sociology
  • Political science
  • Education (teacher training)
  • Nursing
  • Engineering
  • Environmental studies

Our Objectives:

  1. To provide a safe, caring, and supportive environment for all students.
